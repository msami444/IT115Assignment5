A Ferry Expedition

Exploring the world’s waterways offers unique perspectives through iconic ferry rides, such as New York’s Staten Island Ferry, which provides a free, 25-minute journey showcasing Manhattan’s skyline and the Statue of Liberty. The Golden Horn Ferry in Istanbul is another must-see, navigating the Bosphorus River and connecting Europe and Asia with views of Istanbul's historical architecture, including minarets and Ottoman mansions. In Hong Kong, the historic Star Ferry, operating since 1888, crosses Victoria Harbour, offering passengers stunning views of the bustling city skyline. Finally, the Washington State Ferry in Seattle takes passengers across the scenic Puget Sound, revealing the majestic Mount Rainier, the Seattle skyline, and the Olympic Mountains, making it an unforgettable experience.
